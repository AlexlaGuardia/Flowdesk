# Stampwerk Competitive Analysis

## HoneyBook (honeybook.com)
> Researched: 2026-03-31

### Pricing (post-Feb 2025 hike)

| Plan | Monthly | Annual (mo) | Old Price | Hike |
|------|---------|-------------|-----------|------|
| Starter | $36 | $29 | $19/mo | +89% |
| Essentials | $59 | $36.75* | $39/mo | +51% |
| Premium | $129 | $81.75* | $79/mo | +63% |

*Annual pricing shows 25% off on some pages; exact annual pricing varies by source.

Existing users got 20% off new rates for 1 year (applied Feb 4, 2025).

**Stampwerk at $12/mo is 3x cheaper than HoneyBook Starter ($36) and 5x cheaper than Essentials ($59).**

### Transaction Fees (same across all plans)

| Method | Fee |
|--------|-----|
| Visa/Mastercard | 2.9% + $0.25 |
| Amex/Discover | 3.4% + $0.09 |
| Card-on-file (autopay) | 3.4% + $0.09 |

| Cash/check | Free (manual) |

No per-plan fee differences. Premium ($500K+/yr) may negotiate lower card rates.

### Features by Tier

**Starter ($36/mo):**
- Unlimited clients & projects
- Invoices & payments
- Proposals & contracts
- Calendar
- All professional templates
- Client portal
- Basic reports
- Up to 2 lead forms
- HoneyBook AI
- NO automations
- NO scheduler
- NO team members
- NO white-labeling ("Powered by HoneyBook" shown)

**Essentials ($59/mo) — adds:**
- Scheduler
- Automations
- QuickBooks Online integration
- Up to 2 team members
- Up to 10 lead forms
- SMS reminders
- Remove "Powered by HoneyBook"
- Standard reports

**Premium ($129/mo) — adds:**
- Unlimited team members
- Priority support
- Multiple companies
- Advanced reports
- Unlimited lead forms

### Key Features

- **Proposals:** Drag-and-drop builder, branded templates, service selection by client, combined proposal+contract+invoice in one "smart file"
- **Contracts:** E-signatures (type or draw), legally binding, smart fields
- **Invoices:** Itemized, payment schedules, autopay option, gratuity toggle
- **Scheduling:** Calendly-like booking (Essentials+ only), Zoom integration
- **CRM/Pipeline:** Visual pipeline tracking, project management, task management
- **Client Portal:** Activity tab, Files tab, Payments tab, Notes tab — clients can view shared docs, pay invoices, message. Cannot create files or schedule meetings through portal.
- **Automations:** Workflow builder with triggers (Essentials+ only) — emails, tasks, reminders
- **AI:** "HoneyBook AI" — details sparse, likely AI-assisted content generation for proposals/emails
- **Lead Forms:** Embeddable contact forms (2 on Starter, 10 on Essentials, unlimited on Premium)

### Onboarding Flow

1. Sign up with email or Google
2. Brand setup: upload logo, set colors/fonts, default theme
3. Connect payment processor (bank account via company settings)
4. Template selection from built-in library
5. File migration offered free for existing businesses
6. Automation setup walkthrough

Reviews say: clean visual layout, intuitive for CRM newcomers, templates make it quick. But setup can feel complex for template-unfamiliar users.

### Client-Facing Experience

- **Smart Files:** Combined proposal + contract + invoice in one branded document. Client selects services, signs contract, pays — all in one flow.
- **Client Portal:** Centralized hub with Activity, Files, Payments, Notes tabs. Multi-project navigation if client has multiple projects.
- **E-Signatures:** Click/tap signature line, type or draw. Works on any device.
- **Payment:** Credit card, ACH, Apple/Google Wallet. Autopay checkbox. Gratuity option. Confirmation email with receipt link.
- **Branding:** Your logo, colors, fonts on everything client-facing.

### Mobile App

- iOS and Android apps available
- Can manage projects, invoices, and communication
- **Complaints:** Mobile lacks several desktop features. Email editing, reporting, and advanced features are limited or missing on mobile. Multiple reviewers call it "limited compared to desktop."

### User Complaints & Pain Points

**Price Hike (biggest issue):**
- 89% increase on Starter plan triggered mass exodus
- Trustpilot: 3.5/5 across 570 reviews
- Reddit: photographers calling it "quite the price hike," switching to Pixieset, Dubsado
- In poll of 400+ photographers, 38% chose Dubsado vs 19% HoneyBook

**Transaction Fees:**
- 2.9% + $0.25 per card payment adds up fast for high-volume freelancers
- ACH deposits take 7-8 business days (Square offers next-day)
- Fees described as "hidden" by users who didn't expect them on top of subscription

**Feature Gating:**
- Automations locked behind Essentials ($59/mo) — most freelancers need this daily
- Scheduler also gated to Essentials+ — basic feature locked behind paywall
- Users feel "basic features" are being held hostage

**Support:**
- No phone support — real gap for a platform handling payments and contracts
- Support gaps called out repeatedly on Trustpilot

**Other:**
- Steep learning curve for some
- Mobile app limitations
- US/Canada only (geographic restriction)
- Bloat: AI tools, financial dashboards, lead forms that most users never touch
- Fear of unpredictable future price hikes
