# Stampwerk - ProductHunt Launch Kit

## Product Name
Stampwerk

## Tagline (under 60 chars)
AI proposals + invoicing for freelancers at $12/mo

## Description

HoneyBook hiked prices 89%. AND.CO shut down. Bonsai got acquired with no roadmap. Three freelancer tools died in two months.

Stampwerk replaces the 80% that matters: proposals, contracts, invoices, and follow-ups. At $12/mo instead of $59.

The AI writes full proposals from 5 inputs -- scope, timeline, pricing breakdown, payment terms. Not template-fill. Actual structured output from Llama 3.3 70B.

Accepted proposal? Auto-generates a contract. Signed contract? Auto-creates an invoice with a Stripe payment link. Invoice overdue? A 3-step follow-up daemon sends escalating reminders so you don't have to chase clients.

30-second onboarding. No setup wizard. No onboarding call. Free tier included.

## Maker Comment (first comment after launch)

I built Stampwerk because I was paying $29/mo for HoneyBook and using two features: proposals and invoice reminders. That's it. Two features out of fifty.

Then HoneyBook hiked to $59/mo. AND.CO shut down the same month. Bonsai got acquired by Zoom. Three displacement events in sixty days.

Dubsado is so complex that people charge $500-3,500 just to set it up. That tells you everything about how broken this market is.

Stampwerk does four things well:

1. AI proposals -- answer 5 questions, get a structured proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing
2. Auto-generated contracts from accepted proposals
3. Stripe invoicing with payment links
4. A follow-up daemon that chases overdue invoices in 3 escalating steps

$12/mo. Free tier: 5 proposals and 5 invoices/month. No credit card required.

The AI follow-up daemon is the part nobody else has. Most tools make you manually "send reminder." Stampwerk sends a friendly nudge at 3 days, a firmer reminder at 7, and a final notice at 14. Configurable per client.

Built with FastAPI + Next.js. The AI runs on Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) -- fast inference, low cost, no OpenAI dependency.
